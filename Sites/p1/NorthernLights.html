The Northern Lights

The word "aurora" is derived from the name of the Roman goddess of the dawn, Aurora, who traveled from east to west announcing the coming of the sun.
Ancient Roman poets used the name metaphorically to refer to dawn, often mentioning its play of colours across the otherwise dark sky
